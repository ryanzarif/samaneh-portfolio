Acrylic on canvas

Graduating with a BFA, I worked as an editor, critic, and interviewer in some reputed magazines such as Art Tomorrow which was bilingual, and also Shargh newspaper -the most famous newspaper at that time in Iran. I was stricken by everyday bad news at the heart of newspaper, in addition to social media which had taken the place of traditional media and bombarded people with momentous images of the Arab Spring movement that resulted in the Syrian civil war (2011). This situation motivated me to record those Middle Eastern feelings that could not be adequately expressed by the normal media, or written by reporters or historians. I was to create my artworks in an academic context and continue my researches and find a new phase of expression. So I took that competitive entrance exam again and was admitted to one of the art universities in Tehran. Although it was not easy to handle both my job and my education at the same time, I could undertake my duties very well.
