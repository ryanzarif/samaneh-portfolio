Aesthetics of suffering (2014)

Also, I took photographs of disabled people whoever mostly their disabilities are not seen in their portraits but a sort of suffering is reflected in their faces. These photos were drawn by pencil and were put together to show isolation and prison bars in front of people's faces; I used acrylic and charcoal too.
